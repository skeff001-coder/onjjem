The onJJem Dog Discovery & Merch Engine1. ARCHITECTURE & UI REQUIREMENTSPlatform: Native iOS Application using SwiftUI.Design Language: Luxury, "Encyclopedia" aesthetic. Use deep blacks, gold accents, and high-quality photography.Navigation: A 3-tab system: [Scanner], [My Pack (Gallery)], and [onJJem Shop].2. THE AI DISCOVERY ENGINE (The "Scanner")Function: Integrate an Image Recognition API (Gemini or OpenAI Vision).Data Points: When a dog is identified, the app must generate a dynamic "Profile" including:The Origin Map: An interactive MapKit view showing the specific country/region where the breed originated.Evolutionary Path: A timeline tracing the dog from the Ancient Eurasian Wolf to its current breed group (e.g., Gun Dog, Pastoral, Toy).Deep Care Guides: Full text modules on: "How to safely cut nails," "Cleaning the nose leather," "Paw pad hygiene," and "Identifying signs of worms/parasites."3. REVENUECAT MONETIZATION (The "Impulse Unlocks")Integration: Connect to RevenueCat for In-App Purchases.Paywall Strategy: After a user performs a "Free Scan," display a high-conversion paywall with three specific buttons:Button 1 (49p): "Unlock Ancient Lineage Report" (Historical & Wolf DNA data).Button 2 (99p): "Unlock Masterclass Grooming" (Detailed hygiene & video-style guides).Button 3 (£1.49): "The Complete Breed Blueprint" (Full access to all health, nutrition, and history data).Logic: Use RevenueCat’s SDK to check if a user has "Entitlements" before showing the deep information pages.4. ONJJEM & BAGS OF LOVE INTEGRATIONThe Merchandise Bridge: Connect the app to the Contrado (Bags of Love) API.The User Flow:User selects a photo of their dog from the "My Pack" gallery.User selects a product: Personalised Dog Ball, Canvas Print, or Tote Bag.The app sends the photo and order details via a POST request to the Contrado API.Branding: All shop pages must be branded as "onJJem – Powered by Bags of Love."5. DATA & STORAGEDatabase: Use PostgreSQL (on Replit) to store a history of the user’s scans, the breeds identified, and the custom photos they’ve taken.Photo Vault: Integrate a secure gallery where users can save and "heart" their favorite dog photos for future printing.6. STEP-BY-STEP BUILD ORDER FOR AGENTInitialize a SwiftUI project with a Camera capture interface.Setup the API connection for Breed Identification.Build the "Encyclopedia" views with the specific "Knowledge Beyond Reason" content.Configure the RevenueCat paywall with the 49p, 99p, and £1.49 tiers.Build the onJJem Storefront and connect it to the Contrado API for fulfillment.Critical Tips for Success in Replit:The Secrets Tab: Once the Agent starts, it will ask for API keys. Click the "Secrets" (lock icon) in Replit and add: REVENUECAT_API_KEY, CONTRADO_API_KEY, and GEMINI_API_KEY.The Visuals: If you have photos of your actual onJJem products, you can drag and drop them into the Replit file tree, then tell the Agent: "Use 'dogball.jpg' as the image for the Dog Ball product in the shop."
